Gifts


"Ev Duzme" is a Turkish tradition that roughly translated to "putting the house together". Generally, the essentials are bought and "put together" prior to moving into our new home: living room set, bedroom set, dining room set, silverware, dishes, cooking tools, etc. The smaller needs are then purchased after moving in and noticing what's missing! These can include lamps, office material, mixer, etc. This is the reason that we aren't including a wedding registry, as the items that are bought and "put together" can vary and aren't finalized as of yet. Moreover, the gifts that you provide would be a continuation of this tradition and also contribute towards completing the needs within our house and putting in the final touches!

Thank you so much ahead of time!
